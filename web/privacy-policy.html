Privacy Policy

Integrity Tools by Integrity Specialists

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Introduction

Integrity Specialists ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Integrity Tools mobile and web application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, phone number, company, position, location, and profile photo
  • Inspection Data: Reports, field logs, method hours entries, defect analysis data, and photos
  • User-Generated Content: Notes, calculations, locations, and certifications
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser information, and app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, screen views, navigation patterns, and performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Integrity Tools application
  • Process and store your inspection reports and calculations
  • Enable AI-powered defect analysis and photo identification features
  • Authenticate your account and ensure security
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your feedback and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve performance
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Google Cloud Platform):

  • Data is stored on servers located in the United States
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Authentication provides secure user authentication
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We implement regular security audits and updates
  • However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With your employer: If you are using the app as an employee of a company, your company may have access to inspection reports and data you create
  • Service Providers: We use Google Firebase/Cloud services to host and process data
  • AI Processing: Defect analysis uses Google Vertex AI (Gemini) with your submitted defect data and photos
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition

5. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View and export your data from within the app
  • Correction: Update your profile and information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data (some data may be retained for legal compliance)
  • Portability: Export your inspection reports and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable certain features like analytics tracking

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Personal profile data is deleted immediately
  • Inspection reports and user-generated content are deleted within 30 days
  • Some aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytics
  • Backups may retain data for up to 90 days

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following technologies:

  • Local Storage: To enable offline functionality and cache calculator tools
  • Firebase Analytics: To track app usage and performance (can be disabled)
  • Service Worker: To enable progressive web app (PWA) features
  • Authentication Tokens: To maintain your logged-in session

8. Children's Privacy

The Integrity Tools app is intended for professional use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you are under 18, you must have authorization from your employer to use this app.

9. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using Integrity Tools, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and acknowledge that data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Sending an email notification for material changes (if applicable)
  • Requiring re-acceptance for significant changes
